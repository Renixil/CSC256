Dead Together

How to Play

  • Characters: Reyna (Perceptive, adaptable) & Arthur (Strong, relentless).
  • Morale: Your moral standing. Choices can raise/lower it.
  • Companion Trust: Keep allies on your side; actions affect trust.
  • Time & Location: Tracked at the top; story reacts to it.
  • Bag: Bottom-left. Opens inventory.
  • Settings: Bottom-right. Restart, return to game, or go Home.
  • Stat Checks (d20): Only appear on choices that require a roll. Click to roll; meet or exceed the DC to succeed.
  • Dialogue: Left Click the dialogue below to progress and make decisions.
